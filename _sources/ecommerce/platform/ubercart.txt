Ubercart (Drupal) Store Integration
-----------------------------------

After setting up a Curebit account, you need to create a site for each store you
wish to integrate. Then, download the `Ubercart Curebit Checkout Module`_.

1. Extract the module file in your *sites/all/modules/* directory.
2. Log in to your Drupal site and click
   **Administer** |rarr| **Site Building** |rarr| **Modules**.
3. Under **Other**, check the *Enabled* box for **Curebit checkout** and click
   **Save Configuration**.
4. Click **Administer** |rarr| **Site Configuration**, then click
   **Curebit checkout**.
5. Enter your Curebit Site ID. You can get this from your Curebit dashboard
   after you log in and create a site.
6. Click **Save configuration**.

.. _Ubercart Curebit Checkout Module: https://www.talkable.com/ubercart/curebit_checkout-6.x-0.0.2.tar.gz
