BigCommerce Store Integration
-----------------------------

Curebit is easily integrated into your BigCommerce store.

1. Login to your store's control panel.
2. Click on **Settings** and then select **Conversions** from the drop down menu.

   .. image:: /_static/img/bigcommerce-settings.png
      :alt: BigCommerce Settings

3. In the **Conversion Tracking Code** box, enter the code below:

   .. include:: /partials/note_sample_code.rst

   .. include:: /samples/ecommerce/platform/bigcommerce.rst

4. Click **Save**.
5. You will see the popup below:

   .. image:: /_static/img/bigcommerce-popup.png
      :alt: BigCommerce Pop-up

6. Click **OK**.
