Ubercart (Drupal) Store IntegrationÂ¶

After setting up a Curebit account, you need to create a site for each store you wish to integrate. Then, download the Ubercart Curebit Checkout Module.

  1. Extract the module file in your sites/all/modules/ directory.
  2. Log in to your Drupal site and click Administer → Site Building → Modules.
  3. Under Other, check the Enabled box for Curebit checkout and click Save Configuration.
  4. Click Administer → Site Configuration, then click Curebit checkout.
  5. Enter your Curebit Site ID. You can get this from your Curebit dashboard after you log in and create a site.
  6. Click Save configuration.
