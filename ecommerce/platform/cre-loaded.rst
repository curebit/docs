CRE Loaded Store Integration
----------------------------

1. Download the `CRE Loaded Curebit Add-On`_ package and unzip it to your
   CRE Loaded catalog directory (eg /var/www/creloaded).
2. Login to your administrator console (eg https://yourstoredomain.com/admin)
   with your administrator credentials.
3. Click on **Modules** |rarr| **Add-Ons**.
4. Click on **Curebit Checkout**.
5. Click **Install Module**.
6. Click **Edit**.
7. Enter your Curebit Site ID. You can get this from your Curebit dashboard
   after you log in and create a site.
8. Click **Update**.

.. _CRE Loaded Curebit Add-On: http://curebit.com/creloaded/curebit_checkout_0.0.3.zip
