Zen Cart Store IntegrationÂ¶

  1. Download the Zen Cart Curebit Add-On and unzip it to your Zen Cart catalog directory (eg /var/www/zencart).

  2. Login to your administrator console (eg http://yourstoredomain.com/admin_console) with your administrator credentials.

  3. Click Modules → Order Total.

  4. Click Curebit Checkout.

  5. Click install.

  6. Enter your Curebit Site ID and enable by selecting true.
    You can get your Curebit Site ID from your Curebit dashboard after you log in and create a site.

  7. Click Update.
