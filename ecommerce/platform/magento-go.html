Magento Go Store IntegrationÂ¶

Download the Magento Go Curebit file and follow the instructions below.

  1. Open curebit-plugin.js file in your chosen text editor.
  2. You will need to replace “YOUR-TALKABLE-SITE-ID” with the Site ID assigned to you on your Talkable dashboard after you log in and create a site.
  3. Login to your Magento Go Admin Area.
  4. Go to Design → Themes Editor.
  5. Click Customize on your current theme.
  6. Click on JavaScript Editor.
  7. Click Browse Files, select the modified curebit-plugin.js file and press the Upload Files button.
  8. Click the Save button.
