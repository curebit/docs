CRE Loaded Store IntegrationÂ¶

  1. Download the CRE Loaded Curebit Add-On package and unzip it to your CRE Loaded catalog directory (eg /var/www/creloaded).
  2. Login to your administrator console (eg https://yourstoredomain.com/admin) with your administrator credentials.
  3. Click on Modules → Add-Ons.
  4. Click on Curebit Checkout.
  5. Click Install Module.
  6. Click Edit.
  7. Enter your Talkable Site ID. You can get this from your Talkable dashboard after you log in and create a site.
  8. Click Update.
