Platform IntegrationÂ¶

Curebit is easy to integrate into your eCommerce website. Stores using Magento, Shopify, osCommerce, Ubercart, Opencart, Zencart, Demandware and many more can simply include our extension or application. Select your shopping cart on the left.

For other platforms or custom stores, all that is required is the addition of a javascript tag on your final checkout receipt page (post transaction). Check out the custom integration instructions.

When your customer completes an order, Curebit will determine if they are redeeming an offer or if there are any available deals on the products the customer has purchased.
