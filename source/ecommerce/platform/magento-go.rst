Magento Go Store Integration
----------------------------

Download the `Magento Go Curebit file`_ and follow the instructions below.

1. Open *curebit-plugin.js* file in your chosen text editor.
2. You will need to replace "YOUR-CUREBIT-SITE-ID" with the Site ID assigned
   to you on your Curebit dashboard after you log in and create a site.
3. Login to your Magento Go Admin Area.
4. Go to **Design** |rarr| **Themes Editor**.
5. Click **Customize** on your current theme.
6. Click on **JavaScript Editor**.
7. Click **Browse Files**, select the modified *curebit-plugin.js* file and
   press the **Upload Files** button.
8. Click the **Save** button.

.. _Magento Go Curebit file: http://curebit.com/magentogo/curebit-plugin.js
