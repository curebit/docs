Extra¶

Allows to configure additional options for each view.

Delayed email sending¶

Editor provides an option to delay sending of some emails.

Delay email sending by specifying value of Send email in (hours).

Multiple delays

Email views that support multiple sending delays: Friend Claim Email.

Format of multiple delays: comma separated, e.g. 0, 24, 72 — email will be sent immediately, then in 24 hours, and finally in 72 hours.

See Variables page for details how to use delay variables in order to differentiate email template for specific delay.
