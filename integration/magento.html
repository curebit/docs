Magento Integration¶

Talkable offers free extensions for Magento 1.x and Magento 2.x that allow you to integrate and use Talkable with your Magento store. Please follow the installation instructions for your Magento version:

  • Magento 1.x (version 1.5 – 1.9)

  • Magento 2.x (version 2.0 or greater)

To check your Magento version, navigate to your store’s admin panel. The version number will be listed in the page footer.
